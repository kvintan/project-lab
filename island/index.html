One of the first detailed picture of Isle of Berk taken by the founder “Hiccup”

This is taken at 894 AC, when the first ZenDragon Academy was created

In 894 AC, after the intense Fight at "Dragons Island" against the fearsome Great Red dragon, Hiccup and other founders established the Academy of Dragons on the Isle of Berk.
It was Named ZenDragon.

Hiccup as one of the founder, hope that with the academy being born, many dragons can be trained and help defend the home he loves. This Academy is served as a beacon of unity and defense for Berk,where dragons were taught to become protectors of their homeland, ushering in an era of harmony and cooperation.

The ZenDragon Academy, originally situated near the south of the Isle of Berk due to villagers' unease with dragons, eventually relocates to the island's center after 12 years. This move is made to facilitate the integration of well-trained dragons into the city and to ensure easier access for them to protect Berk effectively.

As of now, the ZenDragon Academy is one of the Isle of Berk's most crucial components, playing a pivotal role in the island's prosperity and defense.
